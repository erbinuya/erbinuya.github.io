What is Unemployment and How Does it Affect Us?

How do we understand Unemployment?

Unemployment is a global issue that affects numerous places and countries all over the world. We must know that there are lots of different factors that makes a person unemployed, such as poverty. Sometimes people choose to be unemployed, and sometimes people weren’t given many opportunities to have a job in the first place. Unemployment has a significant effect in the economy of different nations, as well as the people themselves. Once people become unemployed, the country will be the one providing the necessities and facilities they will be needing. To pay more of these accommodations, the government will start to borrow money, and will eventually increase the debt that the country has. Another effect of unemployment is the effect to a person’s mental health. It can lead to depression, it can cause self-depreciation, and it can induce stress, especially if that person is in need of work for some specific reasons, let’s say in order to feed his family.

Unemployment also plays a key role in the criminal activities present in the country. Being unemployed means a person doesn’t have a job, and that also means that they won’t be able to have the money to provide the needs of their family. So, they would commit illegal crimes in order to provide for their family. All in all, unemployment affects us in a large or a small scale, economically or mentally. And if we do find people who are unemployed, we must try to find ways to help them. It is a disease that has infected the world, and we must find to find the cure in order to help save lives from experiencing these kinds of problems.
